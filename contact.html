Frequently Asked Questions

What are your pricing plans?

ArchitectPro is completely free to use. We offer our core 3D visualization platform at no cost to help architects bring their designs to life.

Is there a free trial available?

Since our service is entirely free, you get full access to all features immediately upon signing up. No credit card required.

How long does it take to render a 3D plan?

Depending on the complexity of your construction plan, our cloud servers typically process and generate the interactive Unity environment within a few hours.

Do I need a high-end computer to view the 3D models?

Not at all. Our cloud-based simulation farm handles the heavy lifting, allowing you to seamlessly view complex 3D projects directly in your browser on most standard devices.

Do you offer training and support?

Yes! We provide comprehensive onboarding, training materials, and 24/7 technical support.
